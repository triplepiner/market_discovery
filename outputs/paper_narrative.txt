TITLE SUGGESTION:
  "GP-Enhanced Sparse Regression for Financial PDE Discovery:
   A Systematic Comparison with Application to Real Market Data"

ONE-SENTENCE CONTRIBUTION:
  "We show that Gaussian Process derivative estimation extends the
  practical noise tolerance of SINDy-based financial PDE discovery from
  under 1% to over 20%, and apply the framework to discover governing
  equations from 1,374 real option contracts."

DIFFERENTIATION FROM FENG ET AL. 2025:
  Feng, Lin, Matlia & Serdarevic (NeurIPS 2025 Workshop on Generative AI in
  Finance, arXiv:2511.08606) recover the Black-Scholes BSDE from real AAPL
  time-series data using stochastic SINDy under the risk-neutral measure.
  Our work differs along three axes:
    (1) DATA: cross-sectional option surfaces (strike x maturity) for
        SPY/QQQ/AAPL/MSFT — 1,374 contracts — vs. single-stock trajectories.
    (2) MODEL CLASS: deterministic PDEs (BS + Dupire local-vol) via
        derivative-form SINDy, vs. stochastic BSDEs via integral-form SINDy.
    (3) METHODOLOGY: the first systematic comparison of 6 derivative
        estimation strategies (FD, SavGol, neural/autograd, GP/analytical,
        spectral/FFT, weak-form) for financial PDE discovery, evaluated
        with R²(clean) — separating fit quality from coefficient accuracy.
  We also contribute the R²(clean) vs R²(noisy) distinction (showing neural
  SINDy's fit quality and coefficient accuracy diverge under noise), and a
  misspecification diagnostic via spurious-term activation on Merton data.

KEY RESULT #1 — GP DERIVATIVE ESTIMATION:
  GP achieves R²(clean) = 0.9199 at 10% noise vs 0.8164 for SavGol
  (best classical alternative). At 20% noise: GP = -0.2862, SavGol = 0.6499,
  Weak = 0.8316. This makes PDE discovery practical at real-market
  noise levels.

KEY RESULT #2 — HARD-CONSTRAINT PINN:
  Original put PINN: rel L2 = 48.04%, R² = 0.5988
  Hard-constraint put PINN: rel L2 = 4.98%, R² = 0.9959 (boundary err = 0)
  Log-price put PINN: rel L2 = 3.69%, R² = 0.9978

KEY RESULT #3 — REAL MARKET DATA:
  Analyzed 1374 real option contracts across SPY/QQQ/AAPL/MSFT.
  Best SPY R²: 0.9624 (GP-SINDy)
  Standardized library reduces condition number from 3.33e+06 to 6.14e+01.
  Volatility skew successfully recovered: SPY OTM-put σ = 29.7%, ATM σ = 16.0%.
